Main causes
🔥
Fossil fuels
Burning coal, oil & gas releases CO₂ — the largest single driver.
🌳
Deforestation
Forests absorb CO₂; clearing them releases stored carbon.
🚜
Agriculture
Livestock & rice paddies emit methane; fertilisers release N₂O.
🏭
Industry
Cement, steel & chemical production add significant emissions.

Key effects of climate change
🌡️
Extreme heat
Heatwaves are more frequent, intense & longer-lasting worldwide.
🌊
Sea level rise
Ice sheets melt; oceans expand — threatening 1 billion coastal residents.
🌿
Ecosystem loss
Coral bleaching, species extinction & habitat collapse accelerating.
⛈️
Severe storms
Hurricanes & cyclones gaining intensity; rainfall more unpredictable.
🌾
Food security
Crop yields at risk as droughts and floods disrupt agriculture.
🫁
Human health
Disease vectors spreading; heat illness & air quality worsening.
